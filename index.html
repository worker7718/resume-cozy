Website Care Basics

A practical course for anyone who has been handed a finished website and wants to keep it up to date without a developer.

Learn how to make small copy and image updates, check the important moving parts, keep your pages tidy, and spot the moments when it is time to call someone in.

Format
Self-paced lessons
Made for
Non-designers
Includes
Monthly care checklist
Request syllabus Request syllabus Preview lessons
  1. 01

    Basic navigation.

    Find your dashboard, pages, media, logins, and the few settings worth knowing by name.

  2. 02

    Make small changes gently.

    Update words, swap photos, and keep spacing, buttons, and headings from drifting out of tune.

  3. 03

    Check the pathways.

    Test menus, links, forms, mobile views, and checkout or booking steps before visitors find them.

  4. 04

    Keep the shelves tidy.

    Refresh services, projects, files, and seasonal notes without turning your site into a storage room.

  5. 05

    Care for the hidden things.

    Understand simple backups, updates, domains, analytics, and the quiet checks that keep a site steady.

  6. 06

    Know when to ask for help.

    Notice red flags, gather useful notes, and make the next designer or developer visit easier.
